DINOSAUR

Chaser Game

Timmy the tiny-armed T-Rex keeps tripping over evil chickens. Every time he trips over a chicken his health goes down, but those darn chickens keep reproducing!

Help Timmy the T-Rex stay on his feet by avoiding the chickens for as long as you can and squashing their eggs when they appear. Move your mouse across the screen to move Timmy around.

To pause at any time, press the spacebar.
